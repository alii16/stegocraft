Key Features

Discover the powerful features of StegoCraft, your go-to tool for advanced steganography.

Multiple File Types

Hide data in various file formats including images (PNG, JPG, GIF), audio files (MP3, WAV), and video files (MP4, AVI) to suit your specific needs.

Strong Encryption

Add an extra layer of security with AES-256 encryption, ensuring that even if the steganographic method is detected, your data remains protected.

Advanced Algorithms

Choose from multiple steganography techniques including LSB (Least Significant Bit), DCT (Discrete Cosine Transform), and DWT (Discrete Wavelet Transform).

Text Steganography

Hide messages within plain text using various text-based steganography methods including whitespace encoding, zero-width characters, and homoglyph substitution.

Cross-Platform

Works on any device with a modern web browser. No installations required - simply upload your files and get started immediately.

Privacy-Focused

All processing happens in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security for your sensitive information.
